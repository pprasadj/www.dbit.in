ADMISSION 2020-21

ADMISSION FOR FIRST YEAR OF FOUR YEAR –FULL TIME DEGREE COURSE IN ENGINEERING
  • Eligibility for Admission:
    Eligibility criteria as prescribed by the Government of Maharashtra and given in the Admission Rules. Kindly refer to website http://www.dte.org.in/ Every student seeking admission to the First Year of Engineering should register themselves with the Centralised Admission Process (CAP) conducted by DTE, Maharashtra State at the DTE facilitation Centres (FC). This Process is pre-requisite and mandatory also for Minority students seeking admission for Minority Category.
  • CAP Seats :
    29% seats are filled through CAP conducted by DTE.
  • Minority Seats :
    51% seats are reserve for Minority candidates (Roman Catholic Community).
  • Institute Level Seats :
    20% seats are allotted as Institute Quota, Candidates who wish to avail of Institute Level Seat of the college, first must register at the DTE Facilitation Centre , procure a merit number and only then apply at Don Bosco Institute of Technology for the Institute seat.

  • For latest updates of admission process check the college web site regularly (www.dbit.in). Telephone: 022-68878700
