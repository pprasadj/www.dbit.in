Training and Placement Cell of DBIT

About T&P Cell

DBIT Traing And Placement Cell strongly assimilates the St Don Bosco message to the world i.e. "Help Each Other". We have a student centric approach and we make certain that we connect with as many companies as possible for the placement of our students. Our institute has been blessed with abundance of talent and our Alumni, placed in the industries, have fared very well and are successful in their corporate journeys.

DBIT Training and Placement cell is recognised by industries for providing the best trained students. Our placement policy is holistic and well balanced making us a prominent choice for the companies who wish to hold placement drives.

A dedicated and strong T&P team along with the right infrastructure and resources enable us to carry out pooled campus placements efficiently. We are open for placement activities 365 days 24X7 and we assure the Industry, the Students and the Parents that we will not leave any stone unturned to make all our stakeholders happy and proud.

Campus placements play a pivotal role in shaping up the career of any student. Most students dream of getting placed in a top organization and we are committed to providing them the required opportunities to fulfill this and other aspirations.

Recruiters usually prefer candidates who are ready for the industry and have the professional etiquettes necessary for the corporate world. During campus placements, recruiters test for an array of skill-set in their potential employees. In addition to being knowledgeable with regards to their core subjects, students are also expected to possess great aptitude, reasoning abilities and a set of soft skills. We at DBIT regularly organize numerous training programs to sharpen the skills of our students.

List of Training Programs offered :

  • Communication Skills Training.
  • Aptitude Training (Specific to Third Year Students).
  • Interview Preparation Session (for Third Year and Final Year Students).
  • Mock GD / PI Sessions( for Third Year and Final Year Students).
  • Mock Aptitude Tests -Written and Online(for Third Year and Final Year Students).
  • A Dedicated Language Lab for Improving student's Language Skills.
