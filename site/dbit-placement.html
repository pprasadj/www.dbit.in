About T&P Cell

DBIT Traing And Placement Cell strongly assimilates the St Don Bosco message to the world i.e. "Help Each Other". We have a student centric approach and we make certain that we connect with as many companies as possible for the placement of our students. Our institute has been blessed with abundance of talent and our Alumni, placed in the industries, have fared very well and are successful in their corporate journeys. DBIT Training and Placement cell is recognised by industries for providing the best trained students. Our placement policy is holistic and well balanced making us a prominent choice for the companies who wish to hold placement drives.
