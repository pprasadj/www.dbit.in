Courses Offered

Computer Engineering

Equipped with state-of-the-art software and highly qualified faculty members, our Computers Department is one of the best in the city. DBIT’s lucid and highly engaging teaching methods have helped turn talented, inquisitive students into industry ready professionals.

Know MORE

Information Technology

Our I.T department, complete with the best software amenities and resources, houses faculty members who are experts in their respective fields. Blockchain, deep learning, AI and cryptography are some of the many extremely poignant disciplines taught in our I.T courses.

Know MORE

Electronics & Telecommunication

Our EXTC department houses fully-functional electronics labs complete with the latest equipment and highly trained lab professionals. Our various, engaging Electronics courses embody the concept of hands-on learning.

Know MORE

Mechanical Engineering

The department of Mechanical Engineering, DBIT marries technical prowess to state-of-the-art laboratories and workshops that help students gain hands-on experience. Complete with expert faculty and an extremely courteous lab staff, the department provides an extremely engaging insight into the world of physical engineering.

Facilities at DBIT

Library

DBIT’s library holds a rich collection of more than 34,794 texts as well as reference books in various domains. Providing access to the best weapons of the mind to enthusiastic students, the library sees to it that it's kept updated and rejuvenates students constructively.

Technical Laboratories

DBIT has various laboratories in order to allow students to experiments with his ideas. DBIT has more than enough computer labs, Electronics and Mechanical labs. We also have special labs like robotics, advanced computing machines for high end machine learning computations.

Sports Facilities

DBIT encourages and promotes various sporting activities to inculcate sportsmanship qualities of leadership, perseverance, team spirit and brotherhood along with developing numerous cognitive skills through participation in various sports activities!

Entrepreneurship Cell

Entrepreneurship cell in DBIT is a student's organization dedicated to promoting the spirit of entrepreneurship among students. The basic aim of E-Cell is to encourage college-level students today to start their own enterprise.

National Service Scheme

DBIT has a separate NSS unit to provide its contribution to society. NSS regularly conducts events like blood donation camps, Tree plantations, Water Harvesting and many more activities.

Moodle

DBIT has been using Moodle for Teaching and learning for many years. Teachers share learning material and conduct tests and accept assignment submissions on moodle.
