Courses Offered

Computer Engineering

Equipped with the means to learn in depth about distinct technological and computerised fields around which the whole world has begun to revolve, DBIT aims to incentivize Computer Engineering by pushing students to transform into technically competent individuals with authentic theoretical as well as practical knowledge.

Information Technology

With the intention of providing a strong and a cemented background to each and every technologically inclined function and to store, receive, retrieve data to significantly catalyse communication and efficiency in data processing, DBIT flourishes students in the field of Information Technology to revolutionise virtualization and telecommunication.

Electronics and Telecommunication

Protecting the importance of the contribution of technological resources ranging from the defence sector, exceptional yet rare space programs to the basic internet and the entertainment industry , DBIT helps emphasise the significantly wide scope that the branch of Electronic and Telecommunications Engineering comes with as it provides different and numerous platforms for researches by the students.

Mechanical Engineering

Establishing technological dominance as it solely pertains to applying researched and tested technologies in order to make human life easier, efficient and effective, the department of Mechanical Engineering in this Institution teaches students extensively about manufacturing already existing technologies and to utilize them to bring strengthened reformations in the world.

Facilities at DBIT

Library

LDBIT library holds the rich collection of more than 34,794 text as well as reference books in various domains. It has subscribed to 26 print journals of ‘National’ as well as ‘International’ repute; additionally it has subscribed to the E-Resources such as ‘DELNET’ under which campus wide access to more than 929 e-Journals, 1000+ NPTEL are available for the users.

Technical Laboratories

DBIT has various laboratories in order to allow students to experiments with his ideas. DBIT has more than enough computer labs, Elecronics and Mechnical labs. We also have special labs like robotics, advanced computing machines for high end machine learning computations.

Sports Facilities

DBIT encourages and promotes various sporting activities to inculcate sportsmanship qualities of leadership, perseverance, team spirit and brotherhood. It also helps holistic development of psychometric and cognitive skills of the student through effective participation in the various sporting activities of the college

Entrepreneurship Cell

Entrepreneurship cell in a DBIT is student's organization dedicated to promoting the spirit of entrepreneurship among students. The basic aim of E-Cell is to encourage college-level students today to start their own enterprise.

National Service Scheme

DBIT uses NSS to provide its contribution to the society. NSS regularly conducts events like blood donation camps, Tree plantations, Water Harvesting and many more activites.

Moodle

Moodle is World's best popular Learning Management System. DBIT has been using moodle for Teaching Learning from many years. Teachers share learning material and conduct tests and accept assignment submissions on moodle.
